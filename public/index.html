FAQ

Everything here seems a little basic. Why is that?

Good observation! While I strongly believe in the appeal of mainstream web design, I chose to emphasize accessiblity for my personal website. Why? I want it to be as friendly as possible to as many people as possible! By no means is this a perfect attempt, mind you. I just want to promote mindfulness for others. Feel free to audit this page and give me feedback.

What technologies are you most interested in?

Too many to count! I love learning about emerging technologies and seeing how they can be applied to real world scenarios. I also love modern programming languages and implementations such as Kotlin and Rust and Deno.
